What is Dnd?

DnD or "Dungeons and Dragons" is a classic role playing game played with pen and paper. Currently in its 5th iteration, Dungeons and Dragons consists of one Dungeon Master narrating and crafting the story of the game and several Player Characters living out roles within the Dungeon Masterâ€™s world. It is easy enough for children to learn, but complex enough to take the most seasoned veterans years to master.

What do you need to play?

  • The Rulebook
  • An Adventure
  • Pencil and Paper
  • An Imagination
  • Roleplaying Dice

Who can play?

Dungeons and Dragons can be played and enjoyed by anyone, young or old. However, specific roles must be filled to play a game. One player must take the role of Dungeon Master while the other players must fulfill the roles of Player Characters. So long as you have at least one Player Character and only one Dungeon Master, you can play. (Though the game is more fun with more people!)
