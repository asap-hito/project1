Players

The Player Character

Most players take on the role of Player Character. They choose the actions of one character within this world and. through the use of roleplaying dice, determine how successful each action is. They interact with one another as well as the Dungeon Master to move the story forward and carry out whatever quests they have set upon.

The Dungeon Master

The Dungeon Master, or DM, is responsible for controlling the entire world and every other character in it. They do this by utilizing the Rulebook as well as having an intimate knowledge of the adventure th Player Characters are embarking in. A great deal of quick thinking and a rigid application of the rules are required by DM's in order to run the game successfully.

Working Together

The Player Characters work in concert with one another to accomplish a common goal. That much is obvious. What might not be as obvious, however, is that they also work together with the DM. Nobody is working against anyone else. The DM doesn't win if the Players lose. Everybody wins when everyone else is having a good time. That's what makes this game truly magical.
