Equipment

The rulebook, now known as the Player Guide can be used in concert with the Dungeon Master's Guide, and Monster Manual to help the Dungeon Master guide the game and the players understand the rules.

Adventures can be purchased, taken from generous creators on countless websites, or even created by the Dungeon Master themself. Either way these are the worlds that the players play in and the general plot that they follow.

To play the game you need a Character Sheet with special locations to put in your character's stats. This represents how skilled a character is and change as they learn and grow. You may also want to take notes.

Imagination is key. While this game can be played with large grid-based maps and detailed miniatures, the entirety of the game takes place in the collective imaginations of the Dungeon Master and Player Characters.

These special dice determine everything in the game and include the common six-sided die, down to a four-sided pyramidal die and up to a twenty-sided die. These are key to the gameplay itself.
